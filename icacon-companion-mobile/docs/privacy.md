# Privacy Policy — ICACON 2026 Companion

**Last updated:** 2026-07-22  
**App:** ICACON 2026 Companion (`com.icacon.companion`)  
**Contact:** icacon2026@gmail.com  

## Overview

ICACON 2026 Companion is a free event information app for the ICACON 2026 conference in Aligarh. It is designed to work offline and does **not** require users to create an account.

## Data we collect

**We do not collect personal data.**

- No account or profile  
- No analytics or advertising SDKs in version 1.0  
- No tracking across apps or websites  
- No sale of data  

## Information on your device

- Conference programme text, contacts, and PDF documents are **bundled inside the app** for offline use.  
- Opening a PDF may use your device’s share sheet or a PDF viewer you already have installed.  

## External services

If you choose to open:

- the official website,  
- online registration, or  
- other links listed in the app,  

those pages are provided by third parties (for example icaconaligarh.com or the registration provider). Their privacy policies apply when you visit them. The app only opens those links when you tap them and when a network connection is available.

## Phone and email

Tapping a phone number or email address hands off to your device’s Phone or Mail app. We do not process those communications.

## Children’s privacy

The app is a professional conference companion and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy if the app’s behaviour changes (for example if analytics are added in a future version). The “Last updated” date will change accordingly.
