## Full description

ICACON 2026 Companion is the official-style mobile companion for the 7th International & 17th National Conference of the Indian College of Anaesthesiologists (Aligarh, 11–13 September 2026).

**What you get offline**
• Workshop list and workshop-day schedule  
• Conference brochure PDF  
• Day 1 and Day 2 programme PDFs  
• Secretariat and registration contacts (tap to call or email)

**Also available when online**
• Official website, registration, and venue pages

**Indoor navigation**  
JNMC indoor map is marked Coming soon and will ship once confirmed floor plans are available.

No account required. No ads. Built for on-site use with limited connectivity.
